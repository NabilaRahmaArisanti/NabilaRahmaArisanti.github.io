Spaghetti Bolognese

Spaghetti is a type of pasta that is long, thin, cylindrical, and dense, which, if you look closely, resembles noodles in general. It is a staple in traditional Italian cuisine. Because they are shaped like noodles, most people call them noodles from Italy although many other pasta variants are shaped like noodles. One of the variants of the dish is spaghetti bolognese.In some countries, spaghetti is sold in cans with sauce.

About

Spaghetti Bolognese consists of spaghetti served with Bolognese sauce made of tomatoes, minced beef, garlic and other spices. Sometimes minced beef can be substituted for other minced meat.

Bolognese sauce is a meat-based sauce in Italian cuisine, typical of the city of Bologna. The original recipe is a slow-cooked sauce, and its preparation involves several techniques, including saut√©ing, dry-saucing, and braising.

Video

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Water
  • Minced meat
  • Seasoning (sugar, salt, chili powder, seasoning oil)
  • Sausage

Step

  1. Boil noodles and complementary ingredients in 400 cc (2 cups) of boiling water for 3 minutes while stirring.
  2. While the noodles are simmering, mix the spices, chili powder, and seasoning oil in a plate.
  3. Drain the noodles, then mix the noodles into the spice mixture on the plate, stir until evenly distributed.
  4. Cut the sausage, then place it on the spaghetti.
  5. Delicious spaghetti ready to serve.
